Low and Slow

posted by BGB, november 15, 2016

this week i am extremely excited about a new cooking tecnique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, cooking for grreks,jeff potter describes it as "ultra-low-temperature poaching"
